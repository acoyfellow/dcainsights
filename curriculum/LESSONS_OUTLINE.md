## COURSE 3: Tax-Optimized DCA (45 minutes, 3 lessons)

### LESSON 1: Tax Fundamentals for DCA (15 min)
**Topics**: Capital gains, tax brackets, why DCA helps
**Content**:
- Short-term gains (taxed as income)
- Long-term gains (15-20% for most)
- Why >1 year holding matters
- DCA benefit: More long-term holdings

**Actionable Outcome**: Calculate own tax liability

---

### LESSON 2: Account Type Strategy (15 min)
**Topics**: 401k vs IRA vs Taxable, optimization
**Coverage**:
- 401(k): Pre-tax, match, limited choices
- IRA: Roth vs Traditional
- Taxable: No limits, annual taxes
- 2024 limits: $7K IRA, $23.5K 401k
- Prioritization strategy

**Actionable Outcome**: Know which account to fund first

---

### LESSON 3: Year-End Planning (15 min)
**Topics**: Tax-loss harvesting, wash sales, rebalancing

- Tax-efficient rebalancing
- 2025 contribution limits
